Features

Nepal, country of Asia, lying along the southern slopes of the Himalayan mountain ranges is a landlocked country located between India to the east, south, and west and the Tibet Autonomous Region of China to the north. Its territory extends roughly 500 miles (800 kilometres) from east to west and 90 to 150 miles from north to south.
